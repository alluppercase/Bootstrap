Music

Daniel has been an active musician for pretty much his entire life. As a performer, he started performing at the age of 5 on the violin, and then also learned piano, guitar, drums, and many other instruments. In high school he started learning low brass instruments, and decided to study low brass performance in college at Boise State University. Daniel also has enjoyed writing music, and is always eager to write more music in different styles.




Games

Daniel has also been very interested in video games for most of his life. While his passion started while playing the Super Nintendo Entertainment System, he has been playing ever since. As a college student, Daniel decided to join Boise State's GIMM program (Games, Interactive Media, Mobile Technology). Here he has found many important skills for development and game design, and will continue to during his duration at BSU.
